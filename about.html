About Me

Hi, I’m Dani George, a passionate Full Stack Developer and student with a keen interest in both technology and business. I’m a fast learner who thrives in environments where I can apply my technical skills while constantly expanding my knowledge. As I continue to grow, I’m excited to connect with professionals who share similar interests and explore opportunities that align with my passion for tech and business. Let’s connect and see how we can collaborate!
